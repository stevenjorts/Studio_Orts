BFA Fine Arts Open Studios
Thursday, March 17, 2016 at 6:00 pm to 9:00 pm
Parsons East Building, 4th floor
25 East 13th Street, New York, NY 10003
The BFA Fine Arts program is hosting an OPEN STUDIOS event on March 17th from 6-9pm in the Senior Studio spaces located at 25 East 13th Street, 4th floor.
ALL ARE WELCOME.
Come see what our fine arts students are working on as they approach their bfa thesis shows. Works in progress involving painting, sculpture, photography, performance, installation, textiles and video will all be on display for the public this one night only. This event is in conjunction with the MFA Open Studios taking place on the 5th floor of 25 East 13th Street during the same hours.
Sponsored by Parsons School of Design.
